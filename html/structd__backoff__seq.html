Detailed Description

Backoff sequence (opaque)

Used to generate a sequence of uint32_t backoffs with user-defined semantics (e.g., numbers of microseconds for delaying RPC retries). See d_backoff_seq_init and d_backoff_seq_next for the algorithm.
