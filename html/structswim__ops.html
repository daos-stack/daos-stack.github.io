swim_id_t(* get_dping_target(struct swim_context *ctx)

Retrieve a (non-dead) random group member from the group management layer to send a direct ping request to.

NOTE: to ensure time-bounded detection of faulty members, round-robin selection of members is required.

Parameters
[in]ctxSWIM context pointer from swim_init()
Returns
ID of selected direct ping target, SWIM_ID_INVALID if no available target
